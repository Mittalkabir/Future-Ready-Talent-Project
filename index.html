Relaxing Apartment

Constructed in 1902 by the Belgian King, Leopold II, it truly deserves its place in the most beautiful houses in the world list. Located at the ever-so heavenly French Riviera, its beauty is unmatched. The Villa La Leopolda is built on land that was once owned by the Kings. The property has served as a beautiful backdrop in several notable movies including one film by Hitchcock. The villa is built on a sprawling 18 acres of land and offers a breathtaking view overlooking the beautiful verdant valley. Though in comparison to other same-league properties it has only 11 bedrooms and 14 bathrooms, it compensates well with a beautiful green cover which requires 50 gardeners' efforts to maintain.
